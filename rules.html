📚 Ladder Rules & Guidelines

Welcome to the official rules page for the A’s & B’s Snooker Ladder. All players must follow these guidelines to ensure fair competition and smooth match play.

🎱 Match Format

  • All ladder matches are best of 3 frames.
  • Players must record all frames, points, and high breaks.
  • .

🎯 Challenges

  • You can challenge up to 3 places above you on the ladder.
  • If you win, you swap positions with the defeated opponent.
  • You can challenge more than 1 player at a time.

⏱ Inactivity

  • Players who do not play for 21+ days may be temporarily dropped.
  • Admins will notify players before adjustments are made.

💬 Disputes & Conduct

  • All disputes should be addressed to the club admin.
  • Good sportsmanship is expected at all times.

If you're unsure about any of the rules, feel free to reach out to the committee for clarification.
